About Me

Hi, my name is David Holmes.

I was born and raised in Melbourne, and it's where I live and operate from to this day.

At the age of 22, I decided that academia wasn't the direction I wanted to head in and embarked instead on a journey to learn to code by enrolling in the Coder Academy Fast Track Developer bootcamp.

This website, while still a work in progress, is a testament to the high level of practical knowledge and ability imparted by the instructors at the Academy.

While I do love to code, I also have a very broad variety of other areas of interest, including philosophy, psychology, literature, physics, mathematics, nutrition and exercise... There are too many to list.
